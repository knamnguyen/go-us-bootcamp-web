About the Program

What is Go-US?

Go-US Entrepreneur Bootcamp is a 2-day intensive workshop funded by the YSEALI Reciprocal Exchange Program, sponsored by the U.S. Department of State.

We're bringing an American entrepreneur to Vietnam to share real insights about entering the US market — consumer behaviors, go-to-market strategies, and what it actually takes to succeed.

🎯

US Market Deep-Dive

Understand consumer behaviors, regional differences, and entry strategies

🚀

Go-to-Market Playbook

Social media (TikTok, Instagram, UGC), LinkedIn personal branding, partnerships, community building, building in public, sales process & traditional channels

🏆

Pitch Competition

Present your US market strategy and get feedback from experts

🤝

Network & Connect

Meet founders who've done it, plus like-minded young entrepreneurs
